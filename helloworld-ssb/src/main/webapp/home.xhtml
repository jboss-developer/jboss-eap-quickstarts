This example demonstrates a singleton session bean that maintains state information for 2 variables: incrementA and incrementB.

A counter is incremented when you click on the link to the variable name. If you close and restart your browser, or if you have multiple browsers, you can see that the counter always increments the last value. These values are maintained until you restart the server. To test the singleton bean, click on either "Increment A" or "Increment B" below.
